Jihwan Myung
I am an associate professor at Taipei Medical University (TMU) in Taipei City. I belong to the faculty of Graduate Institute of Mind, Brain and Consciousnessness (GIMBC). I lead the Laboratory of Braintime, with a human lab at Shuang Ho Hospital, and animal and molecular biology labs at TMU main campus. I am also a visiting researcher at Computational Neuroscience Unit, Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology Graduate University (OIST).
